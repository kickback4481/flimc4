Meet

Dr. M

Dr. John Monhollon, MD

Dr. John Monhollon, MD — widely known as "Dr. M" — brings more than two decades of experience in functional and integrative medicine. His approach focuses on uncovering the underlying causes of health conditions and empowering patients to take an active role in their care.

At FLIMC, patients are welcomed with trusted experience, heartfelt compassion, and the highest quality of care.

Over the years, Dr. M has built a team of dedicated professionals who share his commitment to discovering the root cause of health concerns and creating personalized care plans. Together, they have created a center where clinical expertise, compassion, and hope come together to help patients improve their health, restore vitality, and enhance quality of life.
